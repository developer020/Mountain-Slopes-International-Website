Our Curriculum

At Mountain Slopes International, we pride ourselves on offering a comprehensive British curriculum that sets the stage for a world-class education:

Subjects

Immersed in the curriculum, students engage with a comprehensive range of subjects that form the cornerstone of their academic journey. Mathematics, Sciences, Languages, and Humanities are meticulously integrated, fostering a holistic understanding of core disciplines.

Structure

Aligned with the British curriculum's progressive nature, our approach emphasizes structured learning pathways. From Key Stage 1 to 3, students progressively build knowledge, skills, and competencies that culminate in internationally recognized qualifications, preparing them for the challenges of higher education.

Method

Within the context of the British curriculum, personalized learning flourishes. Our educators leverage the flexibility of the curriculum to tailor teaching methods, catering to individual learning styles. This empowers students to explore their interests while meeting rigorous academic standards.

Our Focus

At Mountain Slopes International, our mission is crystal clear. We're dedicated to nurturing essential skills that go beyond traditional learning. We've carefully designed our curriculum to lay a strong foundation in:

Critical Thinking

Fostering a love for inquiry and exploration, we ignite your child's ability to think critically. Through thoughtful challenges and engaging activities, we cultivate their analytical skills, enabling them to approach problems with insight and ingenuity.

Problem Solving

Equipping your child with the tools to unravel complexities is at the heart of our approach. We encourage them to tackle challenges head-on, refining their problem-solving skills that will serve them well throughout their academic and personal journeys.

Creativity

Imagination knows no bounds at Mountain Slopes International. We provide the canvas for your child to express themselves freely, nurturing their creative thinking and pushing the boundaries of innovation. Our environment encourages them to explore, create, and bring their unique ideas to life.
